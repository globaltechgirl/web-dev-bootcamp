Celebration Church International

Celebration Church International (CCI) is a mission-minded teaching ministry founded in 2012 by Apostle Emmanuel Iren. Our vision is to see all people celebrate endless life in Christ Jesus and fully embrace the power of His death, burial, and resurrection.

Our Approach

Celebrating endless life in Christ Jesus demands that you:

Know it

At Celebration Church, the word of God is emphasized to ensure every member knows and understands the lordship of Jesus, and experiences progress and joy in the faith.

Live it

Your life should reflect the death and resurrection of Jesus Christ every single day. It’s not enough to simply know that Jesus is Lord—you must live out that truth in everything you do.

Tell it

Celebrating the finished work of Jesus means sharing it with the world. We are called to boldly proclaim His love and grace to as many people as we can.

Our Lead Pastor, CCI Global

Apostle Emmanuel Iren is the visionary founder and Lead Pastor of Celebration Church International (CCI), a fast-growing global ministry with over 15,000 members and more than 30 campuses in Nigeria, UK, Canada, and USA. He is renowned for his dynamic preaching, teaching, and gospel songwriting. His mission is to help people discover and confidently live out their God-given purpose through transformative power of the Gospel. Apostle Iren’s ministry began during his time as an undergraduate at Covenant University in 2008, where he founded Triumphant Youth Fellowship. This fellowship evolved into what is now CCI, a global church with a strong emphasis on teaching, missions, and empowering believers to fully express their faith in all aspects.
